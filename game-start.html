Controls


Welcome to the Dungeon maze, The rules are simple, explore different rooms in the dungeon, fight monsters and collect their treasures.To win the game you must find the key and defeat the dungeon boss.


Press the dungeon button when you first start the game and after you defeat a monster to move to the next room


When you have a potion, press this button to heal yourself back to your maximum hit points


When you encounter a monster, press the attack button to try and hit the monster


You can also press the block button to try and block the monsters attack, there is a higher chance that your block will be sucessful.However, you can't do any damage this way
